## LEI SYSTEM CONTEXT

**What is a Legal Entity Identifier (LEI)?**
- A 20-character alphanumeric code assigned to legal entities participating in financial transactions
- Governed by the Global Legal Entity Identifier Foundation (GLEIF)
- Required for entities participating in regulated financial markets worldwide
- Recorded in a publicly searchable global database
- Used by banks, regulators, courts, and financial institutions to verify entity identity

**What does Trust LEI Registration mean for this case?**
